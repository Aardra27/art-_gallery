Starry Night Over the Rh么ne

Artist: Vincent van Gogh

Year: 1888

Description: Painted in Arles, this stunning piece captures the starry night sky reflected over the Rh么ne River. It showcases Van Gogh's fascination with night scenes and his innovative use of color and texture.
