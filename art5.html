Starry Night Over the Rhône

Artist:

Vincent van Gogh

Year of Creation:

1888

Description:

This painting captures the view of the Rhône River under a starry night sky. It is one of Van Gogh's most celebrated depictions of night, created in Arles, France, highlighting his fascination with the night sky and reflections of lights on water.
